Hanyu is the first Japanese male skater to win Olympic gold and the first man
since 1948 and 1952 Olympic Chammpion Dick Button to win back - to - back Olympic gold medals.
He is the first skater to perform a clean quadruple loop in competition.
Hanyu became the first skater to surpass a total score of 300 points.
Hanyu followed his older sister Saya into skating.
